# Automated Disaster Recovery & Business Continuity for Large E-commerce Enterprise

## A large e-commerce enterprise handling ₹5–₹8 crore in daily transactions relies on manual backup and recovery processes, resulting in recovery times (RTO) of 2–4 hours and potential data loss (RPO) of 30–60 minutes during failures. With frequent infrastructure issues and database inconsistencies, each major outage causes ₹50–₹80 lakhs in revenue loss and damages customer trust. Their current setup lacks automated disaster recovery, cross-region replication, and regular failover testing, while operating under a ₹60–₹80 lakhs/month cloud budget. If not resolved, prolonged downtime and data loss risks could lead to ₹5–₹10 crore annual losses, compliance violations, and long-term brand damage.
