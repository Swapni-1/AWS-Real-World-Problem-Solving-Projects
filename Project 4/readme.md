# Comprehensive Observability Across Fragmented Environments for large-scale Saas Enterprise

## A large-scale SaaS enterprise running across AWS, Kubernetes, and third-party services generates 500+ GB of logs and metrics daily, yet lacks centralized observability, making it difficult to detect and resolve issues in real time. Their fragmented monitoring setup leads to 30–45 minute incident detection delays and 1–2 hour resolution times, impacting 2–5 million active users and causing ₹20–₹40 lakhs in losses per major incident. With over 100 microservices and multiple teams, they struggle with visibility gaps, alert fatigue, and inconsistent tracing, all within a ₹25–₹40 lakhs/month tooling budget. If unresolved, recurring downtime and poor performance could lead to ₹3–₹6 crore annual losses, reduced customer satisfaction, and increased churn.
